Brooklyn Broke The Universe

Brooklyn Broke The Universe is a story about Brooklyn Woods, somebody who hasn't exactly had the best life, being ripped out of her home universe and given the chance to explore everything that the multiverse has to offer.

The good, the bad, the beautiful, the ugly, she learns to observe her life from an entirely new perspective, finally finding the peace she's seeked for so long, and appreciating life in a way she never has before.

But this is not done easily.

She faces some challenges along the way, which will remain undisclosed for the time.

Characters

Brooklyn Woods

Age 23 // Universe H-20014

Brooklyn is a very quiet person most of the time.

She was raised in a foster home, and really only had her foster brother, Ash, be considered a friend for most of her life.

She finds music to be her one and only distraction from what she sees as an otherwise mundane life, but will soon learn what she's missing out on.

Terra Woods

Age 23 // Universe H-01182

Terra is a multiversal counterpart to Brooklyn. They were both born into the same family, just under different circumstances.

She's the current lead operator in the Multiverse Observation Center designated to her multiverse cluster. (Cluster H)

She's a very energetic and joyful person, but it leaves you wondering: How did she end up here?
