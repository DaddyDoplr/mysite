About me

Hello! Welcome to 2kDoplr Animation! 2kDoplr Animation is an independent animation studio out of Seattle, Washington.

It is currently run by me, the SOLE artist and animator for every video you've seen thus far!

I'm currently a 22 year old University student who has been learning Blender and general animation on the side for a number of years now. I've dabbled in many different forms of art, whether it be music, 3D, 2D, etcetera, all I know is that I absolutely love to create things!

With 2kDoplr, I aim to create many different stories through the medium of 3D animation! And doing so using Blender, which is widely considered to not be "industry standard," when it is entirely possible that it could be. With the right motivation, fitting Blender into the standard animation pipeline is doable, somebody just needs to do it is all lmao

A lot of weirdos automatically make the connection that animated content would automaticaly make it "for kids." But I want to show that more serious-toned content can be made in an animated form as well! Because after all, animation is not a genre, it's a MEDIUM.

I'm rambling. Sorry lol but you get my point!!
